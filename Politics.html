India to hold G20 summit in September 2023

Bangladesh to be the only South Asian neighbour in the list of “guest countries” for the G20 summit

Last Updated: Dec 14, 2022, 18:26 IST
New Delhi, India

India will hold over 200 G20-related meetings across the country during its presidency of the grouping that will begin on December 1, 2022 and continue till November 30, 2023. The G20 Leaders’ Summit will be held in New Delhi on September 9 and 10 in 2023, and Bangladesh, Egypt, Mauritius, Netherlands, Nigeria, Oman, Singapore, Spain and the UAE will be the “guest countries” at the event, the Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) announced on September 13.
